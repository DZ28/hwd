The High Woodlands Swim Team offers training and practice groups for swimmers of all ages and ability levels. It is the goal of the team to offer age-specific training for all athletes geared towards challenging and developing each individual to the best of his/her abilities.

Practice the first week will determine if your child is ready for the team. Click Here to send us an email request for more information. Workout Days/Times shown below are for the 2022 Summer season.



Age Description Practice Times
8 and Under Swimmers can swim the length of the pool without touching bottom or using the ropes to aid them. They do not need to know all the strokes. Swimmers in this group focus mainly on learning four legal competitive strokes, having fun, and perfecting start and turn technique. Monday thru Thursday 7:00 to 7:30PM until school is out, 9:15 to 9:45AM beginning June 20.
9-10 Swimmers can swim the length of the pool without touching bottom or using the ropes to aid them. They do not need to know all the strokes. Swimmers in this group focus mainly on learning four legal competitive strokes, having fun, and perfecting start and turn technique. Monday thru Thursday 7:30 to 8:00PM until school is out, 8:45 to 9:15AM beginning June 20.
11-12 Swimmers in this group continue to learn four legal competitive strokes, have fun, and perfect start and turn technique. They begin to work on endurance. Monday thru Thursday 8:00 to 8:30PM until school is out, 8:00 to 8:45AM beginning June 20.
13-14 Swimmers in this group continue learning four legal competitive strokes, having fun, and perfecting start and turn technique. They also continue to work on endurance. Monday thru Thursday 8:30 to 9:00PM until school is out, 7:00 to 8:00AM beginning June 20.
15 and up These are the leaders on the team. Many have been with the team since they were able to and can help with the younger kids at meets and practices. Swimmers in this group continue perfecting four legal competitive strokes, having fun, and perfecting start and turn technique. They also continue to work on endurance. Monday thru Thursday 9:00 to 9:30PM until school is out, 6:30 to 7:30AM beginning June 20.
